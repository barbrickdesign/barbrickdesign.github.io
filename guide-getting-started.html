STEP 2: Obtain Security Clearance

If you don't already have one, you'll need a government security clearance (PIV/CAC card):

  • PIV Card: For federal employees and contractors (Common)
  • CAC Card: For military personnel and DoD contractors

Clearance levels include: Confidential, Secret, Top Secret, and TS/SCI. Your clearance level determines which contracts you can access.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: The clearance process can take 3-12 months. Start early! Contact your employer or a government contracting officer for sponsorship.

STEP 3: Complete Registration

Visit contractor-registration.html and provide:

  • Full name and organization
  • Email and phone contact
  • White card number and expiration date
  • Security clearance level
  • Special access programs (if any): NOFORN, NATO, FVEY, ORCON
  • Technical capabilities (select all that apply)
  • Team size and years of experience
STEP 4: Wait for Approval

The System Architect will review your registration and verify your white card credentials. This typically takes 1-3 business days.

You'll receive an email notification when approved. You can then access classified contracts!

STEP 5: Browse & Bid on Contracts

Once approved, visit classified-contracts.html:

  • View AI-matched contracts based on your skills
  • See match scores (Excellent/Good/Fair/Poor)
  • Click "Place Bid" to submit your proposal
  • Choose cryptocurrency (ETH, SOL, USDC, USDT)
  • Enter bid amount, timeline, and approach

üéØ Understanding Your Dashboard

Performance Score (0-100)

Your performance score is calculated from:

  • 30% - On-time delivery rate
  • 25% - Quality of work (client ratings)
  • 20% - Budget adherence
  • 15% - Client satisfaction
  • 10% - Project complexity bonus

New contractors start at 50/100 (New Contractor status). Build your score by completing projects successfully!

Contract Match Quality

  • Excellent (85-100): Highly recommended - great fit
  • Good (70-84): Recommended - strong alignment
  • Fair (50-69): Consider - moderate match
  • Poor (<50): Not recommended - limited alignment

üí∞ Bidding & Payments

Submitting a Bid

  1. Review the contract requirements carefully
  2. Check FPDS market data for pricing guidance
  3. Click "Place Bid" on the contract card
  4. Enter your bid amount in USD
  5. Select cryptocurrency for payment
  6. Provide timeline estimate (in days)
  7. Describe your project approach
  8. Submit bid

When Your Bid is Accepted

An escrow smart contract is automatically created with milestone-based payments:

  • 10% - Project start (initial funding)
  • 40% - Mid-point delivery
  • 50% - Final delivery and acceptance

Funds are released by the client as you complete each milestone. Secure and transparent!

‚ùì Common Questions

Q: Do I need a clearance to register?

A: Yes, you need an active PIV or CAC card with appropriate security clearance. The platform verifies clearance levels and only shows contracts you're authorized to see.

Q: Can I bid on contracts above my clearance level?

A: No, the system automatically filters contracts based on your clearance and special access programs. You'll only see contracts you qualify for.

Q: What if I don't have a team?

A: Solo contractors are welcome! Indicate "1-5" for team size. You can also partner with other contractors for larger projects.

Q: How long does approval take?

A: Typically 1-3 business days. The System Architect verifies your white card credentials and reviews your profile for completeness.

Q: Is MetaMask safe?

A: Yes, MetaMask is one of the most trusted crypto wallets with millions of users. Always keep your seed phrase secure and never share it with anyone!
