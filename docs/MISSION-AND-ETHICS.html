Contract Screening Process:

Every government contract is evaluated: 1. Purpose: Does it serve the common good? 2. Impact: Does it help or harm people? 3. Ethics: Aligned with Catholic teaching? 4. Contract Evaluation Checklist:

Before listing ANY government contract: Purpose Check:
  • [ ] Serves civilian population?
  • [ ] Improves quality of life?
  • [ ] Promotes peace?
  • [ ] Ethical use case?
Impact Assessment:
  • [ ] No harm to innocents?
  • [ ] Environmental impact acceptable?
  • [ ] Benefits outweigh risks?
  • [ ] Aligns with mission?
Financial Ethics:
  • [ ] Fair compensation?
  • [ ] No exploitative terms?
  • [ ] Transparent pricing?
  • [ ] Community benefit?
Spiritual Alignment:
